Frequently asked

How does "Show hotel to driver" work?
Save your accommodation once, then open its taxi card. It shows the hotel's exact Korean address full-screen with the brightness turned up — just hand your phone to the driver.

The map or place search isn't finding my hotel.
Make sure you have an internet connection. You can also type the Korean address manually on the accommodation screen.

How do I remove ads?
Tap the upgrade badge on the Today screen and purchase Korea Triplive PRO — a one-time purchase that removes ads permanently.

I bought PRO but it isn't active / I got a new phone.
Open the PRO screen and tap "Restore purchases". Purchases are tied to your Apple ID and can be restored at any time.

Are emergency numbers real?
Yes — 112 (police), 119 (fire/medical), and 1330 (24/7 tourist hotline with English support) are official Korean numbers. In a real emergency, call directly.
